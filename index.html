We're here to help!

DevDesk Queue is an online ticketing system that serves Lambda students that need help during the course. It gives helpers a way to administer assistance to those students easily and provides a visual way to track their current tasks.

Student or Helper?

How It Works

As a helper you can...
  • Login to your own account
  • View all unresolved tickets
  • Assign open items
  • Close resolved tickets
  • Un-assign your own tickets
As a student you can...
  • Login to your own account
  • Submit a ticket with your issue details
  • Clearly view all unresolved tickets
  • Ability to receive prompt assistance
